  • Visual Shape: Clamp, set screw or shrink locking item for locating parts on a shaft.
  • Common Types: Set screw collars, one-piece/two-piece clamp collars and shrink discs.
  • Key Checks: Confirm shaft size, OD, width, keyway, material and clamping style.
  • Application Clue: Used for positioning, retaining, spacing or locking hubs.
  • Do Not Confuse With: Bearing inner rings or taper bushes.

Application

  • Shaft Positioning: Hold components in place on shafts without machining shoulders.
  • Conveyors and Rollers: Locate bearings, rollers, sprockets, and pulleys on shafts.
  • Automation Equipment: Used as stops, spacers, and locators in moving assemblies.
  • Power Transmission: Shrink rings provide high-clamping, keyless locking for hubs and shafts.
  • Maintenance and Repair: Useful for quick field adjustments and replacement component positioning.
